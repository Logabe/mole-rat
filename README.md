# Mole-Rat

## What is this? 

The mole rat is a mouse, controlled by tilting and connected with BLE. 

### Features: 
  - powered by SEED STUDIO XIAO ESP32-C3
  - MPU-6050 sensor
  - Bluetooth
  - LiPo-battery
  - being attached to your feet

---

## Why? 

Have you ever had the problem of not having enough space and time to enjoy your last seconds before the world ends?? Then mole rat is the thing for you. It saves you a total of 5 seconds by not having to lift your hand and move it to the mouse to click a link. It archieves that by being attached to your feet and connected via BLE. 

---

## How? 

  1. Take the SEED STUDIO XIAO ESP32-C3 out of its box
  2. Get the MPU-6050 out of its bag
  3. Connect the SDA and SCL pins of the MPU to the I2C pins (D4 and D5) and the XIAO
  4. To power the MPU-6050, connect VCC to 3.3V and GND to GND
  5. Solder the LiPo battery to the XIAO
  6. 3d print the case
  7. Put the MPU and the XIAO into the case
  8. Take the 2 switches and mount them inside the case too
  9. Wire them to the ESP
